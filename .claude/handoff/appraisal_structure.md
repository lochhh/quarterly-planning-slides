## A2: Demonstrating achievements
Reflect on your continued professional development and more specifically your achievements across the Technical Professionals Career Framework. Focus on your most meaningful contributions and the impact those have had on your role, your team, and your department.

1. Professional Practices
Summarise your most significant contributions and their impact including activities such as analysis, problem-solving, research contributions, grant work, teaching, training, design, and specialist equipment expertise. This includes developing new solutions, supporting research projects, and improving equipment or machinery knowledge.
2. Interpersonal Skills
Describe how you connect, collaborate, and communicate effectively, including teamwork, networking, supporting others through mentoring or supervision, sharing knowledge through presentations or outputs, and working with service providers, stakeholders, or external partners to build strong relationships within and beyond UCL.
3. Management, Planning, and Delivery
Provide examples of how you’ve demonstrated Management, Planning, and Delivery, including overseeing projects or resources, planning and improving processes, and managing budgets, procurement, and administrative tasks.
4. Governance and Compliance
Describe your contributions to Governance and Compliance, including your contribution to maintaining standards and creating a safe, sustainable, and compliant environment.
5. Institutional citizenship
Summarise your institutional citizenship activities below, highlighting any key achievements or contributions beyond your core role that support UCL’s mission, values, and culture, such as promoting collegiality, wellbeing or Equality, Diversity and Inclusion (EDI), participating in governance or UCL-wide projects, and raising UCL’s profile through engagement, volunteering, or similar activities.
6. Development
Please provide details of any formal or informal development activities undertaken and explain how these have contributed to your professional growth.
7. Feedback
If applicable, please provide a summary of any notable feedback, recognition, or awards received.

## A3: Planning for the future
Use it to outline:
- Your likely priorities for the coming year
- The impact you want your work to have
- Any skills, experience or responsibilities you would like to develop
- Your longer-term career direction
Focus on what will make the greatest difference to your team, department and your own development.

1. Priorities
Consider areas of focus for the coming year to discuss with your appraiser. You will have the opportunity to formally record and set objectives when completing Part B of this form.

2. Career Aspirations
Outline your career aspirations, including any goals around progression, reward and recognition, broadening or deepening of technical expertise, taking on supervisory or leadership responsibilities, or development opportunities. You do not need to have a fully formed plan. This section is intended to support an open discussion about your development and how it can be supported.
